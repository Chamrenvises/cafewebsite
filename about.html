Our Journey

Honoring Khmer Heritage, Fueling GenZ Vibes

Founded in the heart of Phnom Penh, Sabay Café GenZ redefines Cambodian coffee culture. We source organic beans directly from small-holder farms in Mondulkiri, matching traditional slow-drip techniques with experimental modern brewing aesthetics.

We are more than just an aesthetic spot—we are a community hub where creators, students, and digital natives gather to connect over authentic flavors that pay tribute to our roots.

"Coffee isn't just a morning routine; it's a celebration of Khmer culture and connection."
